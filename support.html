Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Leaf Node?

Leaf Node is a platform focused on promoting eco-friendly practices through virtual planting, reward redemption, and community engagement.

How do I redeem my points?

You can redeem your points by visiting the "Buy and Redeem" page, selecting a reward, and filling out the redemption form.

How can I participate in virtual planting?

To participate, go to the "Virtual Planting" page where you can engage in activities and contribute to reforestation efforts virtually.

What should I do if I encounter issues with the site?

If you experience any issues, please reach out to our support team via the "Support" page, or contact us directly through the contact form provided there.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support through the "Support" page by filling out the contact form, or by emailing us at support@leafnode.com.
